Title: Social Media Do’s and Don’ts


Do’s:
1. Use respectful and professional language while posting.
2. Think before sharing any content online.
3. Verify information before forwarding or posting.
4. Maintain privacy settings on your accounts.
5. Use social media for learning and networking purposes.


Don’ts:
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
3. Do not spread fake news or unverified information.
4. Do not engage in unnecessary online arguments.
5. Do not misuse social media platforms for harmful activities.
